About AniList+

Track your favorite anime, share your thoughts, and discover new series—all in one place.

What is AniList+?

AniList+ is a platform designed for anime fans to keep track of what they are watching, what they have completed, and what they have dropped. It allows users to explore anime, write reviews, and discover new series through the community.

Features

  • 📺 Track currently watching, completed, and dropped anime
  • ⭐ Rate and review anime
  • 🔍 Browse and search through a catalogue
  • ➕ Add missing anime to the database
  • 👤 Create and customize your profile

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a simple, clean, and enjoyable platform where anime fans can organize their watching experience and share their opinions with others.
